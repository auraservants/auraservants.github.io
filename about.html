Hello! 🖐

I'm Aura. Nice to meet you!
Digital Designer at @VML

My name is Aura, and I am a digital designer with over 7 years of experience. I specialized in frontend development, another one of my passions. I began my undergraduate studies at the University of Margarita in Venezuela, where I earned a Bachelor's degree in Arts with a major in Graphic Design. I graduated in 2016, and shortly after, in 2017, I moved to Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Since my time at university, I kept myself busy by doing freelance design work of all kinds. During my university internship, I worked at a well-known local agency that had clients from all over the world.

In Buenos Aires, I started working for small agencies and niche industries, where I was in charge and responsible for the design and marketing departments.

During the pandemic, I decided to take a short career course in software development with a specialization in frontend development. I received a scholarship for this course and completed it in 2023. I have always been passionate about web design, which led me to shift my focus to this area.

Currently, I work as a digital designer at VML, formerly known as Wunderman Thompson, a multinational marketing and technology company. Here, I have worked with various global clients such as Kyndryl, Pfizer, Boehringer Ingelheim, Movistar, Philip Morris, and currently, L'Oréal.

Additionally, I take on freelance projects across various fields, including branding, web design and development, email marketing, social media, and more.
